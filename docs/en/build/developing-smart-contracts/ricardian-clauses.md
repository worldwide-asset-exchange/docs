# Ricardian Clauses

Ricardian clauses are reusable pieces of human-readable contract language that help document common meanings, conditions, and obligations across actions.

## Why Use Clauses

- reduce duplication across actions
- keep legal or user-facing language consistent
- make contract documentation easier to maintain

## WAX Builder Guidance

Use clauses to document repeated terms, but keep them aligned with how the contract actually behaves. They should clarify the contract, not drift away from implementation.
