# Marketplaces on WAX

WAX is widely used for NFTs and game assets. Marketplaces are where users typically browse collections, list assets, buy items, and manage parts of their trading workflow.

## What to Check Before You Use a Marketplace

- wallet compatibility
- collection and asset visibility
- support for the asset standard you use
- fees and listing rules
- whether the marketplace is primarily for collectibles, gaming assets, or broader discovery

## Common Marketplace Activities

- browse collections
- buy and sell NFTs
- review asset metadata
- move between a wallet and marketplace inventory
- interact with collection-level tools and launch flows

## Before You Trade

- confirm the collection and asset details
- review the action in your wallet before signing
- understand whether the action is a listing, purchase, transfer, or approval flow
